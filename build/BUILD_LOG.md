# Autonomous Build Log and Assumption Register

**Build date:** 2026-07-12  
**Repository:** `backpackerjohn/Tube-`  
**Operating rule:** No questions were sent to the owner. Ambiguities were resolved through evidence, conservative assumptions and documented reversal conditions.

## Mission interpretation

Build a complete, launch-ready blueprint for a faceless, camera-free, asset-based YouTube channel. Research had to align across vidIQ, the YouTube Data API and broader web sources. Nothing was published, deployed, emailed or sent to another person.

## Orchestration used

The work was divided into independent roles, then reconciled:

1. **Opportunity hunters** examined ten channel concepts from different demand and production angles.
2. **Demand researcher** used vidIQ keyword clusters and channel-pattern discovery.
3. **Viewing researcher** used YouTube Data API results to test recent real viewing behavior.
4. **Market researcher** fetched official, government, manufacturer and high-quality market sources.
5. **Format judge** rejected concepts that structurally depended on custom footage, dangerous repair demonstrations or copied clips.
6. **Commercial judge** assessed purchase intent and monetization fit without inventing revenue forecasts.
7. **Skeptic/red team** attempted to kill the winning thesis and forced revisions.
8. **Completeness critic** checked the master prompt’s definition of done and added the recap, brand assets, templates and decision gates.

These are role-separated passes, not claims that separate human researchers participated.

## Candidate tournament decisions

Ten ideas were scored before selection: used-EV buyer intelligence, home Wi-Fi diagnosis, scam prevention, used-device diagnostics, airport navigation, heat-pump troubleshooting, subscription traps, appliance diagnosis, sleeper-train planning and megaproject explainers.

The selected concept was **used-EV buyer intelligence**. It had the strongest balance of demand, recent viewing proof, commercial intent, topic depth and camera-free originality. Home Wi-Fi was retained as the unrelated reserve niche. Several higher-viewing concepts were rejected because the winning product depended on physical repair or travel footage.

## Tool and source limitations

- vidIQ metrics are connector snapshots without query-specific public URLs. They are retained as directional evidence and graded accordingly.
- YouTube API searches ordered toward high-view results prove that viewing exists, but do not expose the full failure distribution. The package rejects view forecasting from the sample.
- Market datasets cited through Reuters and specialist providers were not independently downloaded when proprietary.
- A first attempted GitHub commit fetch used an incorrect final SHA character; it failed without altering files and was retried with the verified SHA.
- The initial blueprint referenced three SVG brand concepts that did not yet exist. The completeness pass created them before sign-off.
- No live YouTube channel analytics exist yet. The 90-day decision framework makes actual channel behavior the post-launch source of truth.

## Major decisions and why

### Select EV Second Opinion
The strongest opportunity is not generic EV reviews. It is an auditable buying desk using **Verified / Observed / Unknown**, an original evidence score and conditional **Buy / Wait / Walk** verdicts.

### Keep a visible uncertainty product
A faceless channel cannot replace a physical inspection. Every episode must show what can and cannot be known without the exact VIN, diagnostic access, insurance quote or qualified inspection.

### Make original analysis the visual product
Every episode requires a decision matrix, calculation, model-specific diagram, source walkthrough and reversal/unknown card. Stock footage is optional punctuation, never the core value.

### Launch with a ten-video library and three-video buffer
The channel should not be judged from one upload. Ten scripts form a coherent pre-purchase sequence and the buffer protects accuracy.

### Pre-commit the kill switch
The overlooked strategic deliverable is a written Scale / Iterate / Pivot / Kill framework that prevents post-launch goalpost movement.

## Files intentionally not created

- No public channel, website, newsletter, form or social account.
- No outreach list containing real personal contacts.
- No sponsor pitch populated with invented audience numbers.
- No unlicensed footage, music or manufacturer imagery.
- No vehicle-specific diagnosis pretending to replace inspection.
